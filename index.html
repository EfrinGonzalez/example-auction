How KindAuction works

A simple, non-technical journey for busy parents: list, bid, win, and arrange a safe handoff.

1

List item

Add photos, condition, safety notes, pickup area, starting bid, and optional buy-now price.

2

Bid simply

Buyers see a clear minimum next bid and receive friendly validation before a simulated bid is accepted.

3

Win safely

The future product can notify winners, reserve the item, and prepare a secure payment or pickup flow.

4

Arrange handoff

Families agree on public pickup, local delivery, or shipping with dispute support available if needed.
